Child Care ( 2 yrs to 10 yrs)

Peppy Kem is a safe, hygienic and professionally managed home away from home for your child. As working parents you can put in your whole mind in following your passion/profession once you entrust your child in the safe hands of Peppy Kem. The kids will be engaged continuously through various indoor and outdoor games and activities planned in accordance with their age and understanding under safe supervision.

An out of the class and unique After School Program exclusively designed for children forms a part of their day.

We know how precious it is at the end of the day to bond with your child with loads of talking and sharing. We help the child with their school work also so that you and your child will have more free time together at home.

Healthy, nutritious and homemade snack are given to your child before they get on their chosen activities.

Sandwiched between their first moments at Peppy Kem and the late evening departure, they participate in a host of activities. The exciting variety of themes, activities and events are a means to an end. The fun and learning happens along the way!
