Welcome To PeppyKem

Best Education In Our PeppyKem

Peppy Kem meaning Active Home was inaugurated on June 3rd 2013. It’s a venture initiated by Ms Susan, a post graduate in Social work and an HR professional with years of Corporate experience. Her professional experience, artistic flair and love for children on the whole made an amazing combination in developing the idea and running it successfully till day.

Realising the importance of education in the early years of growth of a child, Peppy Kem started Early Learning Program in 2016. Intense research was done on educational systems to frame a curriculum that is best for early education. We follow Montessori based curriculum further developed and modified by inculcating traditional Indian practices and culture. The curriculum and software is supported by Kreedo Early Childhood Solutions. Our intensively trained teachers are Facilitators who create self-learning environment for children.

We believe in providing a safe, hygienic and professionally managed Care Centre for your child. An out of the class and unique After School Program exclusively designed for your child also makes Peppy Kem unique.
